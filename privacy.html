Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Information We Collect

The Salem app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking SDKs, and we do not operate any server that stores information about you. Guide content is bundled with the app or loaded from public APIs. The only time information leaves your device is when you actively use a network feature — weather, maps, walking directions, or the AI chat and tarot features — as described below.

Location Data

The app may request location permission solely to show your position on the map. This data is processed entirely on-device and is never sent to any server. Location permission can be denied or revoked at any time without affecting other app features.

Network Requests

The app makes the following network requests:

  • National Weather Service API — for weather forecasts and current conditions (uses a fixed Salem, MA coordinate, not your device location)
  • OpenStreetMap tile servers — for map imagery; tiles are requested for the area shown on screen
  • OpenStreetMap-based routing (OSRM) — to draw walking trails between fixed points of interest; your device location is not sent
  • Google Gemini API — for the AI chat guide and tarot readings (only when you send a message or request a reading; the text you enter is sent to Google to generate a response, and is not stored on any server of ours)

No identifying information is included in these requests beyond what is necessary for the API to function. Text you send to the AI features is processed by Google under its own Privacy Policy; avoid entering personal or sensitive information into the chat or tarot features. AI-generated responses may be inaccurate and are for entertainment and general information only.

Feedback & Suggestions

The in-app Suggestions feature opens your device's email app with your message pre-filled, addressed to the developer. It is only sent if you choose to send it, and goes directly to the developer's inbox — it is not collected or stored by us otherwise.

What We Don't Do

  • No account creation or sign-in.
  • No advertising SDKs.
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • We never sell data, because we don't collect any to sell.

Third-Party Services

Links within the app open external websites in your device browser. Those sites operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

Children's Privacy

Salem is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, email nikolasjonchambers@gmail.com or open an issue on the GitHub repository.
